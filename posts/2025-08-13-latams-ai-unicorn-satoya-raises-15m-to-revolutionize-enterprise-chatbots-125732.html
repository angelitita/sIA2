Latams ai unicorn satoya raises 15m to revolutionize enterprise chatbots

Publicado por Redacción sIA el 13 de Agosto de 2025 en Noticias

Chile-based Satoya, a leading provider of natural language processing (NLP) and chatbot solutions, has secured a significant $15 million investment from top-tier VCs to fuel its expansion and continue pushing the boundaries of AI in Latin America.

A Chatbot Revolution

Founded in 2018, Satoya's mission is to democratize AI by making sophisticated chatbot technology accessible to enterprises of all sizes. The company has developed an AI-powered chatbot platform that allows businesses to create human-like conversational interfaces for customer service, sales, and marketing purposes.

This Investment

The $15 million funding round was led by prominent investors including Andes Ventures, Global Founders Capital, and Conector. The funds will be used to further develop Satoya's AI technology, expand its sales and marketing efforts, and build strategic partnerships with major corporations.

Breaking Ground in LatAm

Satoya's achievement is a testament to the growing AI ecosystem in Latin America. The region has been witnessing an influx of innovative startups and a surge in AI adoption among large enterprises. With this investment, Satoya is poised to become a game-changer in the regional AI market.

A Bright Future Ahead

Satoya's vision is to empower businesses to create personalized, empathetic, and cost-effective customer experiences. With its cutting-edge AI technology and commitment to innovation, the company is well-positioned to make a significant impact in the global AI landscape. We can expect to see exciting developments from Satoya in the near future.
